Main Prizes

Each sponsor is going to be allowed one or more judges for the event. Each judge will have a certain number of points to assign to teams based on their sponsor level. The teams with the most points win. Prizes will be distributed evenly among the members of winning teams.

First Prize - $1000

Second Prize - $500

Third Prize - $250



RethinkDB Prize

An additional $1000 will be distributed among any placing teams that use RethinkDB in their project.



Mystery Event

There will be a sideline, non-programming event. The judges will again decide your fate.

Mystery Event First Prize - $50/team member on the winning team.
